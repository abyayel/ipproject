Batman Justice Buster

Batman Justice Buster is the title of the new comic that the publisher Kodansha has dedicated to Batman and the first renewed collaboration between the DC and Publishers.Batman: Justice Buster follows the Dark Knight three years after he has begun fighting crime and is meeting most of his iconic villains. Favorite villains - like Two-Face and the Joker - also make appearances, although Joker tends to fall more into the vigilante character than his normal evil self. Although there is a "good Joker," the Jester, that exists in the DC Universe, this version of the Joker is pretty different. One of the most important changes from the traditional Batman is that this Batman is wearing a full suit of armor with an AI named Robin. The Robin AI is more than just a sidekick, though, it's a life-saver.
